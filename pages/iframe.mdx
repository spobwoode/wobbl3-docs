# iFrame Integration

You can embed the widget in your website using an iframe. The widget will be responsive and will adapt to the size of the iframe. This will enable your affiliates to promote your nfts or token on their website.

## 1. Get the widget code

To get the widget code, go to the mint page and click on the "Embed" button.
